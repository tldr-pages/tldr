# xar

> Manage .xar archives.

- Create a xar archive of all files in {{directory}}:

`xar -cf {{archive.xar}} {{path/to/directory}}`

- List the contents of the xar archive sample.xar:

`xar -tf {{archive.xar}}`

- Extract the contents of sample.xar to the current working directory:

`xar -xf {{archive.xar}}`
