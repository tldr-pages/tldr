# locale

> Get locale-specific information.
> More information: <https://man7.org/linux/man-pages/man1/locale.1.html>.

- List the global locale variables and their values:

`locale`

- List all available locales:

`locale --all-locales`

- Display all available locales and their metadata:

`locale --all-locales --verbose`

- Display your current date format:

`locale date_fmt`
