# scriptreplay

> Replay typescript files created by script command.

- Replay {{typescript_file}} at recorded speed:

`scriptreplay {{timingfile}} {{typescript_file}}`

- Replay {{typescript_file}} at double-speed:

`scriptreplay {{timingfile}} {{typescript_file}} 2`

- Replay {{typescript_file}} at half-speed:

`scriptreplay {{timingfile}} {{typescript_file}} .5`
