# lddd

> Find broken library links on your system. This tool is only available on Archlinux.
> More information: <https://man.archlinux.org/man/extra/devtools/lddd.1.en>.

- Scan $PATH, /lib, /usr/lib, /usr/local/lib and /etc/ld.so.conf.d/*.conf to find out packages with broken library links:

`lddd`
