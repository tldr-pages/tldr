# fortune
> Prints a random quote, saying or joke from a collection of fortunes.
> More information: https://manned.org/man/fortune

- Print a random fortune:
  
`fortune`
  
- Print a fortune from a specific fortune file:
  
`fortune /usr/share/games/fortunes/{file}`

- Pipe a fortune into another fun command (like `cowsay` or `lolcat`):
  
`fortune | cowsay | lolcat`
