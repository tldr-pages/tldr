# tuitube

> A TUI for downloading videos from YouTube, X, Twitch, Instagram, Bilibili, and more using `yt-dlp`.
> More information: <https://github.com/remorses/tuitube>.

- Launch the interactive TUI:

`tuitube`

- Download a video from a URL:

`tuitube {{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=example}}`

- Download a video in a specific format:

`tuitube --format {{mp4}} {{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=example}}`

- Download only the audio from a URL:

`tuitube --audio-only {{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=example}}`

- Download a video to a specific output directory:

`tuitube --output {{path/to/directory}} {{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=example}}`

- Display help:

`tuitube --help`
