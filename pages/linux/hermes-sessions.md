# hermes sessions

> Manage session history — list, rename, export, prune, and delete conversations.
> More information: <https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/docs>.

- List past sessions:

`hermes sessions list`

- Browse sessions with an interactive picker:

`hermes sessions browse`

- Rename a session:

`hermes sessions rename {{session_id}} {{new_title}}`

- Export a session:

`hermes sessions export {{session_id}}`

- Prune old sessions:

`hermes sessions prune`

- Delete a specific session:

`hermes sessions delete {{session_id}}`
