# lb

> A blog management tool.
> More information: <https://github.com/LukeSmithxyz/lb>.

- Make a new draft:

`./lb new`

- Edit a draft:

`./lb edit`

- Delete a draft:

`./lb trash`

- Publish a draft:

`./lb publish`

- Delete a published post:

`./lb delete`

- Unpublish a published post to edit it as a draft again:

`./lb revise`
