# funzip

> Print the content of the first (non-directory) member in a zip/gz archive without extraction.
> More information: <https://manned.org/funzip>.

- Print the content of the first member in an archive:

`funzip {{path/to/archive[.zip|.gz]}}`

- Decrypt the archive and print the content:

`funzip -password {{password}} {{path/to/archive[.zip|.gz]}}`
