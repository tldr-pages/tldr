# bottom

> A graphical process/system monitor for the terminal.
> More information: <https://github.com/ClementTsang/bottom>.

- Launch bottom:

`btm`

- Launch bottom in basic mode (no graphs):

`btm --basic`

- Sort processes by CPU usage:

`btm --cpu_left_legend`

- Launch with a specific refresh rate in milliseconds:

`btm --rate {{1000}}`

- Launch in process-killing mode:

`btm --kill_on_ctrl_c`
