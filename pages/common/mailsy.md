# mailsy

> Quickly generate a disposable email using mail.tm API.
> More information: <https://github.com/BalliAsghar/Mailsy>.

- Create Email Address:

`mailsy g`

- Fetch Emails (press Enter to open email in browser.):

`mailsy m`

- Details about Account:

`mailsy me`

- Details about Account:

`mailsy d`
