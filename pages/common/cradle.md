# cradle

> The Cradle PHP framework.
> See `cradle-install`, `cradle-deploy` and other pages for additional information.

- Connect to a server:

`cradle connect {{connection_name}}`

- Call general help:

`cradle help`

- Call help on a specific command:

`cradle {{command}} help`

- Execute a Cradle command:

`cradle {{command}}`
