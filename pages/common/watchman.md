# watchman
> Watches files and runs actions when they change.
> Often used in development for hot-reloading, building, or logging.
> More information: <https://facebook.github.io/watchman/>.

- Start watching a directory:
  watchman watch /path/to/directory

- List all watched directories:
  watchman watch-list

- Stop watching a directory:
  watchman watch-del /path/to/directory

- Shutdown the Watchman server:
  watchman shutdown-server
