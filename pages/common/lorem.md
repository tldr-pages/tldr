# lorem

> Create more or less random lorem ipsum text.

- Print the number of words you want:

`lorem -n {{20}}`

- Print 10 lines of Goethe's Faust:

`lorem -l {{10}} --faust`

- Print 5 sentences of Poe's Raven:

`lorem -s {{5}} --raven`

- Print random words from Boccaccio's Decameron for 40 characters:

`lorem --decamerone --randomize -c {{40}}`
